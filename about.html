About Mandal

Serving Selu With Faith, Unity And Celebration

Our Purpose

MAHESH GANESH MANDAL, Selu brings devotees together for Ganesh Utsav through daily worship, cultural programs, disciplined arrangements and community support.

The mandal is guided by devotion to Lord Ganesha and a commitment to welcoming every visitor with respect, cleanliness and warmth.

Core Values

  • Seva for devotees and society
  • Transparent event coordination
  • Respectful festival management
  • Culture, devotion and togetherness
